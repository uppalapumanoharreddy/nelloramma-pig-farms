About Us

Nelloramma Pig Farms focuses on ethical, sustainable, and scientific pig farming practices with strong emphasis on animal welfare, innovation, and rural development. We are committed to producing high-quality pork products while maintaining the highest standards of animal care and environmental responsibility.

Our farm integrates modern technology with traditional farming wisdom to ensure the well-being of our pigs and the sustainability of our operations. We work closely with veterinarians, researchers, and local communities to promote best practices in pig farming.

Our Journey

2022

Conceptualized Nelloramma Swine Farms with a clear vision to promote ethical, scientific, and sustainable pig farming practices in India.

2023

Launched a pilot breeder farm with 10 breeder units to validate breeding performance, feed efficiency, disease management protocols, and local market acceptance.

2024

Successfully scaled operations to 25 breeder units, strengthened veterinary and feed management systems, and established a reliable market and farmer network.

2025 (Early)

Expanded the core team with professionals in finance, engineering, and swine management; formally incorporated the venture as Nelloramma Swine Farms Private Limited.

2025 (Phase I Expansion)

Initiated development of a 100-breeder integrated piggery project in Balangir District, Odisha, including breeder sheds, fattener sheds, and an in-house feed manufacturing unit.
